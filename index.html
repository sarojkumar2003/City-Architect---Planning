To Work with

City Architects & Planning (CAP) is a renowned architectural firm based in Delhi, India. Established in 2021, CAP specializes in town planning, urban design, structural engineering, and a wide range of services. Under the leadership of the experienced architect R K Sharma, CAP's team is dedicated to delivering top-notch quality, efficiency, and client satisfaction at every stage of the design and execution process. With a strong focus on excellence, CAP is widely recognized for its cutting-edge architectural solutions and proficiency in delivering projects that surpass industry standards.
